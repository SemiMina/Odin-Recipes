Blini (Russian Pancakes)

Description

The recipe for thin pancakes with milk.

Ingredients

  • flour - 100 g
  • milk - 150 ml
  • water - 50 ml
  • egg - 1 pc
  • sunflower oil
  • sugar - 1 tsp
  • salt - 1 pinch

Steps

  1. Add sugar, eggs, oil to warm milk.
  2. Add the sifted flour and mix thoroughly until smooth. The dough should be liquid and lump-free.
  3. Heat a frying pan. Grease with the oil. Fry the pancakes over medium heat until golden for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Then the other side. Place the cooked pancakes on a plate in stacks, brushing each with butter.
  5. Enjoy!
